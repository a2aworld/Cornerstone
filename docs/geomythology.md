# Genesis Challenge — Geomythology

Link human narratives to geological and climatic phenomena.

## Tasks
- Myth parsing & motif classification
- Geo‑entity linking
- Temporal alignment with uncertainty
- Hypothesis testing (ECR, permutation, Monte Carlo)

## Datasets
- Smithsonian GVP, USGS Earthquake Catalog, NOAA NGDC Tsunami DB, Meteoritical Bulletin
- Gazetteers: GeoNames, Wikidata
- Proprietary seed dataset: 9 CSV/KML (premium; ODRL)

See BLUEPRINT.md for detailed architecture and metrics.
